# Public Surface Redesign Master Plan

**Date**: 2026-03-24  
**Source**: [PUBLIC_SURFACE_VISUAL_THESIS.md](/Users/tanmoybhadra/Documents/Mrinmoy_Work/GST_BILLING_SOFTWARE/docs/PUBLIC_SURFACE_VISUAL_THESIS.md)

## Purpose

This plan converts the public-surface visual thesis into an execution sequence for implementation. It covers the branded site, auth entry points, legal pages, and internal admin entry route.

## Goal

Ship a coherent public-facing system that feels premium, disciplined, and distinct from the tenant application while remaining aligned with the current product architecture.

## Phases

### O1 Shared Visual Foundation

- establish typography pair for public/auth surfaces
- redesign shared public shell and navigation
- create reusable full-bleed hero composition
- create reusable auth-shell composition
- establish motion utilities for public pages

### O2 Home And Entry Surfaces

- redesign `/`
- redesign `/login`
- redesign `/reset-password`
- redesign `/admin/login`

### O3 Commercial And Support Pages

- redesign `/features`
- redesign `/pricing`
- redesign `/security`
- redesign `/help`
- redesign `/demo`
- redesign `/contact`

### O4 Legal And Trust Pages

- redesign `/privacy`
- redesign `/terms`
- refine trust/footer/legal navigation consistency

### O5 Polish And Validation

- responsive validation across public/auth pages
- visual QA for spacing, hierarchy, contrast, and motion
- browser validation of all public routes
- content pass for copy consistency and CTA clarity

## Implementation Order

1. O1 shared foundation
2. O2 home and auth/admin entry pages
3. O3 commercial/support pages
4. O4 legal pages
5. O5 validation and polish

## Acceptance Criteria

- the first viewport of `/` feels brand-defining rather than card-led
- public pages share one visual language without looking cloned
- login and admin login feel intentional and clearly differentiated
- legal pages read like document surfaces rather than app cards
- public shell, footer, and CTA language are consistent across routes
