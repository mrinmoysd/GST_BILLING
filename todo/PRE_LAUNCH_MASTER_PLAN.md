Status:

## N2 — Environment-Backed End-to-End QA

Status:

- Planned

---

## N3 — Provider and Platform Integration Validation

Status:

- Planned

Goal:

- validate real billing, webhook, file, notification, queue, and export behavior

---

## N4 — Launch Perimeter Completion

Status:

- Planned

Goal:

- finalize public copy, legal text, and launch-facing support perimeter

---

## N5 — Launch Readiness Signoff

Status:

- Planned

Goal:

- perform the final go/no-go decision with evidence from N1-N4

Do not classify the product as publicly launch-ready until:

- N1 through N5 are complete
